Pacific rice financial model

In this project, i created a financial model for a rice cultivation and processing company "Pacific Rice. The Financial Models include General assumptions, Revenue assumptions, Cost of Goods Sold assumptions, CAPEX assumptions, OPEX assumptions, Workings, Financial Statements and Financial Dashboards. The dashboard visualize the following KPIs "Average Revenue, Average Gross Profit, Average Profit After Tax, Overall Performance, OPEX Breakdown and Probality Ratios". This model also involved scenario analysis with Base case, Best case and Worst case scenarios considered
